Sauce AI brings AI-assisted capabilities to the Sauce Labs platform, enabling you to perform testing and analysis tasks using natural language. Whether you're creating automated tests, investigating application errors, or interacting with Sauce Home widgets through AI assistants, each capability helps reduce manual effort while keeping you in control of the testing process.
